# sijunjung-ai


> **Empowering Indonesian Village Cooperatives with AI-Driven Market Matching.**
> *Built for the Hackathon Digital Cooperatives Expo 2026.*

sijunjung-ai is a "Zero-UI" platform designed to transform traditional Koperasi Unit Desa (KUD) into highly efficient supply chain hubs. By leveraging a simple WhatsApp interface powered by Multi-Agent AI, we eliminate the need for complex ERP systems, allowing cooperatives to seamlessly match their agricultural commodities with national offtakers at the best prices.

---

## 🛑 The Problem
Indonesian agricultural cooperatives often lose their economic leverage due to information asymmetry and complex broker chains. When harvest season arrives, subjective quality grading and a lack of direct access to national buyers result in unfair price cuts. Existing tech solutions (like complex web dashboards or generic ERPs) suffer from low adoption rates among rural farmers and cooperative managers.

## 💡 The Solution: Zero-UI Adoption
Instead of forcing users to learn a new app, we bring the technology to where they already are: **WhatsApp**. Cooperative managers simply send a photo of their harvest and a short text message, and our backend AI agents handle the rest.

### Core Features
*   📱 **Zero-UI Interface (WhatsApp Bot):** No downloads, no complicated forms. Just snap a picture and chat.
*   👁️ **Smart Visual Grading (AI Vision):** Automatically analyzes uploaded photos of commodities to provide an objective initial quality grade, preventing unfair price cuts.
*   🧠 **Smart Data Entry (NLP):** Extracts structured data (commodity type, volume, estimated quality) from casual, everyday chat messages.
*   🤝 **Smart Matchmaker (Semantic Search):** Instantly connects the cooperative's inventory with the specific requirements of national factories/offtakers, ensuring the highest possible profit margin.
